My Trading Rules

  3. Wait for confirmation before entry

  5. Follow the trend

üéØ Trading Strategies

Scalping Strategy

Time Frame: 1m, 5m

  • Use 20 EMA and 50 EMA crossover
  • RSI confirmation (oversold/overbought)
  • Quick entries and exits
  • Target: 5-10 pips

Swing Trading Strategy

Time Frame: 4H, Daily

  • Identify key support and resistance
  • Use Fibonacci retracements
  • Hold positions for days to weeks
  • Target: 50-200 pips

üß† Trading Psychology

Mindset Rules

  • Accept losses as part of the business
  • Don't let emotions control your trades
  • Stick to your plan
  • Focus on the process, not the money
  • Take breaks when feeling overwhelmed

Common Psychological Traps

  1. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) - Wait for your setup
  2. Revenge Trading - Take a break after losses
  3. Overconfidence - Stay humble and follow rules
  4. Analysis Paralysis - Trust your system
