Entry Criteria

🎯 Trading Strategies

☰

Scalping Strategy

Time Frame: 1m, 5m
Indicators: 20 EMA, 50 EMA, RSI
Entry: EMA crossover with RSI confirmation
Target: 5-10 pips
Stop Loss: 5 pips
☰

Swing Trading Strategy

Time Frame: 4H, Daily
Analysis: Support/Resistance, Fibonacci
Entry: Bounce from key levels
Target: 50-200 pips
Hold: Days to weeks

🧠 Trading Psychology

Mindset Rules

  • Accept losses as part of the business
  • Don't let emotions control your trades
  • Stick to your plan
  • Focus on the process, not the money
  • Take breaks when feeling overwhelmed

Common Psychological Traps

  1. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) - Wait for your setup
  2. Revenge Trading - Take a break after losses
  3. Overconfidence - Stay humble and follow rules
  4. Analysis Paralysis - Trust your system

🎯 The 3 Pillars of Successful Trading

1. Risk Management (40%) - Never risk more than you can afford to lose - Use proper position sizing - Always set stop losses

2. Trading Strategy (30%) - Have a clear entry and exit plan - Backtest your strategies - Stick to your system

3. Psychology (30%) - Control emotions - Accept losses as part of trading - Stay disciplined

💡 Remember: "It's not about being right all the time, it's about being profitable over time."
